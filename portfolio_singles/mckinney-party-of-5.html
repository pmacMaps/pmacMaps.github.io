McKinney Party of 5 Website

My wife and I decided during a car ride to start a blog together. I had previously built her a site using Wordpress. This time, however, I wanted something that was cheaper, and did not have the overhead of a database. I had been hearing about JAMstack, and after some research, decided that was the way to go.

The blog website was built using Jekyll, hosted on GitHub, and used CloudFlare to serve out the site. There is a general Blog page that lists all published articles, in addition to category pages.

With the many responsbilities and activities that come with a fulfilling life, we decided to close our blog. However, the site has been archived on this website. The final post was written in early 2021.
